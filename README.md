# 🎊 ITA(Inventory Tracking App) 🎊

Welcome to the Inventory Tracking App!! This is my project web-app for CS4524 class.<br><br>
On this file, reader can view various necessary information related to project in order to know about my ITA, including detailed documentations, visuals, progressively-done changes/revision on code, and different project deliverables.<br><br>
In my application, I'll be creating a system such that will be used to keep record and track the inventory of products specifically dedicated for workers who work in the warehouse or people who work at office- away from the warehouse where the products actually will be stored- but still need to be able to view the inventory of the warehouse so that no invalid sales are made and unavailable products are sold or fulfilled.<br><br>
I will be using Java and mySQL with the aim of building such user interface that will present smooth and simple use-experience for users.

# (Project Proposal)Team Assignments

- **Idea**: I got this idea from the person that I know closely and had a work experience in his business recently. He is a kitchen cabinet wholesaler and I know he has pretty big space to fill with his products. In order to maintain his business, profits, and revenues he must have some sort of tracking system of his inventory where anyone who uses that system would have some sort of control over the inventory and be able to do necessary changes accordingly and anyone who has access to view that inventory or connects that application to their online sales portal could avoid making invalid sales that would put the business into hard situation. Therefore, I think this is a crucial part of the business and developing a simple inventory system could help a lot to small businesses like his 
- **Motivation**: Actual motivation beside what I lightly touched upon on "Idea" part- about this being crucial part of such businesses- is that I wanted to develop such an app where I could connect and practice my major-related skills onto business related real-life example sistuations. I like "business" beside my major being CS, and although both are two different science fields, I loved the idea where I can develop a web-app that can be used in a small businesses.
